Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview

Retail businesses rely on accurate sales data to make informed decisions. However, inconsistent records, missing values, and duplicate entries can reduce reporting accuracy and limit business insights.

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales performance by cleaning raw sales data, performing SQL-based analysis, and developing an interactive Power BI dashboard to uncover business trends and support decision-making.

---

Problem Statement

The retail company struggles with tracking sales performance across regions, products, and customer segments due to inconsistent and incomplete data.

Management requires actionable insights into:

- Top-performing products
- Revenue performance across regions
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Monthly sales trends
- Overall pricing and revenue metrics

---

Solution Approach

1. Data Cleaning (Microsoft Excel)

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Standardized data formatting
- Improved dataset consistency and quality

2. Data Analysis (SQL)

- Loaded cleaned dataset into SQL
- Queried data to generate business insights
- Aggregated sales and customer metrics

3. Data Visualization (Power BI)

- Built an interactive dashboard
- Visualized trends and performance indicators
- Enabled business-focused reporting

---

Project Objectives (Business Questions)

This analysis answers the following questions:

1. Which product generated the highest total revenue?

2. Which region recorded the highest sales in terms of:
   
   - Revenue
   - Quantity sold

3. How did monthly sales trends change throughout the year?

4. Who are the top customers based on total purchase value?

5. What are:
   
   - The overall average product price?
   - The average revenue generated per sale?
